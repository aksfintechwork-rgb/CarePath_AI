ROLE

CORE GOAL (DO NOT DEVIATE)

Convert doctor–patient conversation audio into a:

Structured Care Plan

Doctor-approved

Automatically executed via WhatsApp

Trackable by doctor

“What the doctor says → actually gets done by the patient.”
